Why Us

Voeuller Tours and Travel has been adapting to an ever-changing World for more than ten years. It had to reinvent itself many times, always building on a strong heritage of social responsibility, technical excellence and a family character.

With all those many years of experience we have established good partnerships that enable us to offer services to our clients to their level of satisfactory.

Customer Service

Our customer experience is the fuel that keeps us going and the feedback that we get from them is the Captain of the ship that keeps Voeuller Tours and Travel Consultants International Ltd standing and going forward.

How we ensure good customer experience.

  • We consider customer caring service as our number one job. We are in direct contact with each of our customers. And because of it, we know exactly what you want and can meet your every need.
  • We value each customer’s information and appreciate your every comment to us. We are eager to listen and learn anytime how we can improve our service better.
  • We will make sure that each step of your service is taken into account with personal care to make you satisfied.

Authentic Ugandan Experience

Voeuller Tours and Travel is committed to giving our customers an authentic Ugandan experience not just something staged for tourist. We will take you to exquisite Ugandan destinations wherever possible, not to tourist traps.

Exquisite destinations

Based on our knowledge and customer feedback, we use local guides that belong to the ethnic group in areas we operate from.

This increases employment opportunities to the communities but also allows our clients to immerse themselves within the Ugandan culture.

We are local

As a local tour operator; we get more advantages than the agency outside Uganda. We understand Uganda well and we are familiar with all the destinations we operate from.
